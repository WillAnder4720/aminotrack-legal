Frequently Asked Questions

What is protein quality?

Protein quality refers to how well a protein source provides the essential amino acids your body needs. AminoTrack analyzes the amino acid profile of foods to show you how "complete" your protein intake is.

How do I log food?

You can log food in four ways: describe what you ate in natural language, search our database, take a photo of your meal, or scan a barcode.

What does the ring on the home screen show?

The ring shows your protein intake progress toward your daily goal. The green portion represents "complete" protein (balanced amino acids), while colored segments show which amino acids may be limiting.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore any previous Pro subscription.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your food log is stored locally on your device. When using AI features (text/photo analysis), food descriptions are sent to our servers for processing but are not stored or linked to your identity.
